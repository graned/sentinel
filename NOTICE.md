Sentinel is a community-driven authentication and authorization project.
Its goal is to make robust auth infrastructure more accessible, transparent,
and available to everyone.

Project principles:
- Improvements to Sentinel should remain open.
- The project is maintained in the open and welcomes community contribution.
- The project is intended for legitimate, ethical, and lawful use.

If you use Sentinel and find it valuable, please consider:
- contributing code, docs, bug reports, or reviews
- helping other users in discussions
- supporting the project with a symbolic donation
